**Survey Design Notes (for facilitators):**
- Sections B–E map directly to the **MOJO framework**: Motivation (B), Opportunity (C), Job Capability (D), Outcome (E)
- Section F qualitative responses are designed for NMF topic modeling
- B5 is reverse-scored before analysis: score = 6 − raw response
- G1 adoption stage maps to **Self-Determination Theory (SDT)** stages for intervention design
- Recommended n ≥ 100 for meaningful pattern analysis
